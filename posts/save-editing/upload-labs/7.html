Editing Your Money Count (WIN)

Post #7

Hello and welcome to GHG's guide on how to edit your Money count in Upload Labs! This guide is an official guide that will go through how to edit your money count using GHGEdit with upload labs. This guide is for windows.

Step 1: Finding Your Save File

To find your save file, open up windows file explorer and type %AppData%\Upload Labs. This should open your Upload Labs directory. If it doesn't, try searching online for more info. After this, make it sort by date modified and look for the .dat file that is most recently edited. We recommend opening Upload Labs and moving a node around and then closing it to verify. Usually, your save game is in savegame.dat.

Step 2: Opening Your Save File In The Editor

To open your save file in the editor, go to GHGEdit (More info here and change the dropdown that says "Auto-Detect" to "JSON/Text".

Now press the "Choose save file" button and select your save file.

Step 3: Editing Your Save File

Now that you have opened your save file in the editor, it should look something like this:

If it does, you can proceed with this step (if not, repeat the steps above). Now, use CTRL+F to search for "money" (around 18 on finder tool). Look for one that looks like the one in the screenshot.

Step 4: Exporting Your Edited File

Now you have edited your file, you can export it! Just simply press the export edited save button, change it from savegame_edited.bin to savegame.dat, rename your old savegame.dat to savegame.dat.bakup and then save your new one to the same directory! Now you have your new money count!
